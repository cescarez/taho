Instructions for dogsitters:

Do not trust Taho's adorable kissable face and understanding gaze. She will eat any food you leave unattended for >5 minutes. Please place all foods behind the baby gate in the kitchen.

Things to know:

  • She can be shy/aloof with new people. After a few walks and a few meals, she warms up.
  • Because of the aformentioned shyness, she may be stubborn about going places on walks or getting in the car.
    • Feel free to use lots of treats to bribe her into listening to you. Also pieces of food work great as treats right now because she loves her food -- just please reduce her next meal portion accordingly if you feed her a bunch of treats between mealtimes.
  • She does not like the dog park.
    • Once I lost her because she ran away from me to sit by the car.
  • She is afraid of medium to large high-energy dogs.
  • Her recall (return rate when called) is pretty low. We're working on it, but she's definitely got stray-mentality when given complete freedom.
